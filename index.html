our donate plan

We've divided our donation plan into several actions. Currently, we are emphasizing the affected people's basic needs such as food, shelter, and education for a child

food for a family

The people who're the victim of that horrendous natural calamity need sufficient food, and pure drinking water. Our humanity can provide them those all they need.

shelter for a family

A large number of people are still stuck in the water. They've been living under the sky for a week now. They need us to rescue them and provide a safe shelter .

education for a child

Because of the extreme hit of the flood, children are deprived of their education. This is one of the key plans to ensure education among those children.
